Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2024

This privacy policy applies to the Ludu Hunt app (herein referred to as "the Application") for mobile devices, created by the Developer (herein referred to as "Service Provider").

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address), device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing the Application, the time and date of your use of the Application, and other statistics.

The Application does not require any user account creation, nor does it ask for personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Third Party Access

The Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Below are the links to the privacy policies of the third-party service providers used by the Application:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob

3. Cookies and Local Storage

The Application does not use explicit "cookies". However, it uses DataStore (Preferences) to store game progress locally on your device, such as your coin balance and game settings. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to the Service Provider.

4. AdMob Advertising

The Application displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers (like the Android Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads and for analytics purposes. You can reset these identifiers in your Android device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

The Service Provider does not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us so that we can delete such information.

6. Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information and provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect information processed and maintained.

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes by updating this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about the practices, please contact the Developer via the contact information provided in the Google Play Store listing.
